Refund Policy

Thank you for shopping at CheeseCrave. We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Please review our refund policy below:


1. Eligibility for Refunds:

  • We accept returns and provide refunds only for cheese products that are still wrapped in their original packaging.
  • The original packaging must be unopened and in its original condition.

2. Timeframe for Returns:

  • You must initiate the return process within 10 days of the purchase date.
  • Returns received after the 10-day period will not be accepted.

3. Return Process:

  • To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team at info@cheesecrave.com within the designated timeframe.
  • Provide your order details, including the purchase date and product information.

4. Return Shipping:

  • You are responsible for the shipping costs associated with returning the product.
  • We recommend using a trackable shipping service to ensure the safe return of the product.
  • Shipping costs are non-refundable.

5. Refund Processing:

  • Once we receive the returned product and verify its eligibility, we will process the refund.
  • The refund will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.
  • Please allow for a reasonable processing time for the refund to reflect in your account.

6. Non-Refundable Items:

  • Perishable cheese products that have been opened or partially consumed are not eligible for refunds.
  • Any products that are not in their original packaging or have been damaged due to improper handling or storage are also non-refundable.

7. Exceptions:

  • In the event that you receive a defective or damaged product, please contact our customer service team immediately.
  • We will work with you to provide a replacement or issue a refund, depending on the circumstances.

8. Contact Us:

If you have any questions or need assistance with our refund policy, please contact our customer service team at info@cheesecrave.com. We are here to help!

Please note that this refund policy applies only to purchases made directly through our website. If you purchased our products from a third-party retailer, please refer to their respective refund policies.

By making a purchase on our website, you acknowledge and agree to abide by the terms and conditions of this refund policy.
